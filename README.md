HR Analytics Dashboard

This Power BI dashboard provides actionable insights into employee data, focusing on attrition trends, workforce demographics, and organizational patterns. It highlights key metrics such as attrition rate, average age, salary distribution, and years at the company.

The dashboard enables HR teams to identify key drivers of employee turnover and supports data-driven decision-making to improve retention strategies.

Key Features

Attrition analysis by age, gender, education, and job role

Salary slab-wise attrition insights

Employee distribution across departments

Tenure-based attrition trends

Interactive and dynamic visualizations

Tech Stack

Power BI

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

Power Query (ETL & Data Transformation)

Excel / CSV (Data Source)

Key Insights

Higher attrition observed in specific job roles like Sales Executive and Laboratory Technician

Majority of attrition falls within lower salary slabs (≤ 5K)

Employees with fewer years at the company show higher turnover trends

Age group 26–35 shows the highest attrition

💼 Business Impact

Helps HR teams identify high-risk employee segments

Supports development of targeted retention strategies

Improves workforce planning and decision-making through data insights
